Property Definitions

Dyadic
An image of a Cantor cube.
Polyadic
An image of an Alexandroff cube.
Scadic
An image of a product of compact scattered spaces.
Centered
An image of a space Cen(S) (the space of all centered subcollections of S). The spaces Cen(S)'s are also known as Adequate compact spaces.
Hyadic
An image of a hyperspace H(X) of a compact space X.
W* Image
An image of the space W* of all free ultrafilters on W.

Maps onto the space BW of all ultrafilters on W.
Onto H(BW)
Maps onto H(BW).
Onto 2^W1

Contains BW.

Strong Eber
A Strong Eberlein compact space.
Uniform Eber
A Uniform Eberlein compact space.
Eberlein
An Eberlein compact space.
Corson
A Corson compact space.
Cor
Compact ORderable.
Homogeneous
Homogeneous.
Rigid
A crowded space whose only autohomeomorphism is the identity.

Infinite cpctness number. Does not have an n-ary closed subbase, for any n.

Monolithic.

Chains W

Scattered

Unif Pi Char
